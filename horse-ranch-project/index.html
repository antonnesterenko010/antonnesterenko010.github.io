Welcome to Hidden Creek Ranch

Hidden Creek Ranch is a small, family-owned ranch in Cedar Park, TX. It is a hidden gem, nestled in the sprawling, picturesque Shenandoah neighborhood. Owned and managed by Jolie Vinograd, the ranch offers riding lessons, horse boarding/leasing, camps, birthday parties and merit badge workshops.

Along with the horses, the ranch is also home to Moola the cow, a pair of goats named Boris and Natasha, a few chickens, a band of cats, and a crew of dogs.
