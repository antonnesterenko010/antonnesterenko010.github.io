Meet The Staff

Owner, Instructor - Jolie Vinograd

Jolie is the owner and primary instructor. She grew up in Virginia and has ridden since she was 4. Her primary background is English hunter/jumper. In her youth, she helped form the Ballentrae Pony Club and hosted their own horse shows. After moving to California in 1979, she began riding Western Pleasure. She is qualified to instruct in both English and Western styles and has been teaching riding and basic horsemanship for over 20 years.

She is a member of Certified Horsemanship Association
