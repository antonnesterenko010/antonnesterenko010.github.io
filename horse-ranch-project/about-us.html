About Hidden Creek Ranch

Our History

Jolie and her husband, Nick, bought the 6 acre property in 1994. At the time, it consisted of a dated, single family home, a pump house and a shed. Over the years, Jolie and Nick have renovated the house, added 3 barns, a 100x200 ft lighted sand arena, a round pen, and many species of farm animals.

Currently, the ranch is providing full care board for 11 horses in addition to the 6 schooling horses used for lessons. At this time there are about 25 students ranging in age from 5 to 18 years attending lessons on a weekly basis.

Our Future

The hope is to expand Hidden Creek Ranch into a sustainability farm. It would include an aquaponics garden, a petting zoo, and provide kids with the opportunity to learn animal husbandry skills.
